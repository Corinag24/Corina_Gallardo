Tourism Course

A tourism course provides students with the knowledge and skills to work in the tourism and hospitality industries. Courses cover a range of topics, including:

Key Learning Areas:

  • Management: Marketing, finance, human resources, and other business administration functions.
  • Tourism industry: Travel motivation, environmental factors, tourism organizations, and other industry topics.
  • Cultural aspects: Cultural appreciation, Philippine culture and tourism, and other cultural topics.
  • Planning: Travel agency management, tour arranging and planning, and other planning topics.
  • Research: Research methods and thesis writing.

Tourism courses often include internships, on-site training, and other real-world learning experiences. Some universities also offer programs that focus on specific areas of the tourism industry, such as sustainable food management.

Career Opportunities:

  • Front desk clerk or receptionist
  • Events manager
  • Hotel or resort manager
  • Housekeeper
  • Tour operator
  • Tourism marketing manager
  • Travel agency manager
  • Tourist Information Centre manager

Skills for Success in Tourism:

  • Strong communication
  • Problem-solving
  • Leadership abilities
  • Cultural awareness
  • Market insights
  • Passion for travel and hospitality
  • Diplomacy
