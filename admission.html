Admissions

Join our community at Ilocos Sur Community College and take the next step toward your future.

How to Apply

  1. Visit our Admissions Office to fill out the application form.
  2. Submit required documents (e.g., transcript, identification, photos).
  3. Attend an orientation or interview, if required.
  4. Await confirmation of acceptance from our admissions team.

Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Identification documents
  • Recent 2x2 photo
  • Other program-specific requirements

Application Deadlines

Application Period: June 1 - July 31

Entrance Exam Date: August 15

Orientation Schedule: August 25
