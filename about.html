About ISCC

The college is one whose program is designed for useful and effective learning and aims to improve the quality of living in the Community. It offers practical courses to serve the educational needs of deserving but indigent students. It is designated for the young people of Ilocos Sur and its surrounding areas, there being no barrier for admission by reason of age, sex, nationality, religious belief, or political affiliation. It provides low-cost education as part of the Provincial Government's program of social amelioration.

Our Mission

To develop and empower the Ilocano youth to become change-agents through the provision of quality and accessible education and the provision of equitable services to develop sustainable communities, establish linkages, and enhance institutional development.

Our Vision

By the year 2030, ISCC will be a global catalyst for social change through excellence in instruction, research, extension, and production, anchored in Filipino spirit and Ilocano heart.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We promote honesty and strong ethical standards in everything we do.
  • Respect: We believe in respecting the dignity and worth of every individual.
  • Excellence: We aim to achieve the highest standards in academics and character development.
  • Community: We foster a sense of belonging and collaboration among students, staff, and parents.
  • Innovation: We encourage creative thinking and embrace new methods to enhance learning.
