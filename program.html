KABLAAW | The College of Hospitality and Tourism Management of the Ilocos Sur Community College

Posted on October 28, 2024

KABLAAW | The College of Hospitality and Tourism Management of the Ilocos Sur Community College reaped awards in the 17th Annual Hotel, Restaurant, and Tourism Week and 15th National Food Showdown held at the Baguio Country Club, October 24-26,2024. With it’s theme: "Kusina Filipina: Keep the Momentum...Full Speed Ahead!," this year’s HRT Week cherishes the culinary heritage among the Filipinos while highlighting the dynamic growth and innovation in the hospitality and tourism sectors. It served as a platform to showcase the incomparable and diverse skills and talents among students.
